A NETWORK OF DEDICATED CLINICIANS AND INVESTIGATORS COMMITTED TO ADVANCING ALL ASPECTS OF NEPHROLOGY RESEARCH AND EDUCATION

We are a network of experienced academic and private clinical trial centers that partner with sponsors for the purpose of conducting clinical and translational research that improves patient care.

In addition, we provide a mechanism for conducting ancillary basic science and translational studies with the intent of increasing our understanding of the pathophysiology of renal diseases.
